About Third Opinion Podcast

Welcome to Third Opinion Podcast, a show where we explore Self improvement, Nature, Lifestyle, Diplomacy, Travel, Mental health and more. Our goal as your hosts is to entertain, educate, and connect with listeners through compelling conversations from our diverse perspectives.

Hosted by Talia, Josh, and Valentine, each episode is a blend of humor, insight, and storytelling that will keep you coming back for more.

Talia

Talia brings dynamic energy with a passion for content creation and interaction, making her the voice of the podcast and its originator.

Josh

Josh, the creative genius of the three, adds his unique perspective by discussing a wide view on matters and controversial topics.

Valentine

Valentine's humorous personality and love for conversation keep the tone light and engaging.
