About Third Opinion Podcast

Welcome to Third Opinion Podcast, a show where we explore Self improvement, Nature, Lifestyle, Diplomacy, Travel, Mental health and more.. Our goal as your hosts is to entertain, educate, and connect with listeners through compelling conversations from our diverse perspectives resulting from our everyday lives.

Hosted by Talia, Valentine, and Josh, each episode is a blend of humor, insight, and storytelling that will keep you coming back for more. With weekly updates back to back we promise to deliver fresh new perspectives on the regular.

Talia

Talia brings dynamic energy with a passion for content creation and interaction making her the voice of the podcast and its originator.

Valentine

Valentines funny personality and love for conversation and humor are what make up the heart and life of the podcast.

Josh

Josh the creative genius of the three adds his blend of uniqueness to the podcast content by giving out a wide view on matters and controversial topics
